These responses assume a damping factor of 0.707 and natural frequency of 1 Hz. For other damping factors or natural frequencies, the poles need to be recalculated as follows:

  • p1, p2 = (-2 * pi * f0 * bt) +/- j* (2 * pi * f0 * sqrt(1 - bt**2) )
where
  • p1, p2 = poles
  • f0 = natural frequency
  • bt = dampling factor
