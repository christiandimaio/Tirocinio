Note:

IESE does not list current damping or shunt resistor information, so response for the S21g is given with a calculated shunt resistance that assumes a total damping of 0.7071 critical. Additional parameters needed to customize this response using the link above are as follows:
Fundamental Frequency Mass Open Circuit Damping Intrinsic Sensitivity
2.0 Hz 0.023 Kg 0.61 78.7 V/M/S

This nominal responses is valid when the input resistance of the attached amplifier or datalogger is at least 2 orders of magnitude larger than the total resistance of the disconnected sensor. If this is not the case, total damping will not be 0.7071*critical and the poles will not describe the system accurately. Note that the response for the S21g 2 Hz sensor is the same as for the S31g 2 Hz and G88 2 Hz sensors.
