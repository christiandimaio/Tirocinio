Optional IIR Low Cut Filter:The SmartSolo has an optional Low Cut Infinite Impulse Response (IIR) filter whose low-frequency corner can be set to 0.05 (DC Removal), 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, 8, 9 or 10 Hz, or can be Disabled (Off). The responses given here include the DC Removal, 1 Hz and Disabled options. If you have chosen another setting for this filter, you will need to choose either the 1 Hz or DC Removal response and replace the pole value for this stage with 2*pi*fc where fc is your selected corner frequency.
