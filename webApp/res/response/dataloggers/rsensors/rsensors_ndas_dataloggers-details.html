The NDAS has selectable main sample rates of 1, 10, 50, 100, 125, 250 500 and 1000 Hz. In addition, it can produce a 1 Hz sample rate decimated from the main sample rate. To choose responses for these decimated sample rates, select the Main (nondecimated) Sample Rate and Decimated Sample Rate of 1Hz. Decimated Sample Rates will be listed with their Main Sample Rates in the NRL tables.
