Firmware versions
FIR filters for the 250 and 500 Hz sample rates changed on October 1, 2012. Dataloggers sampling at 250 and/or 500 Hz need to select responses based on their firmware dates.

Long-period sample rates
The 0.1 and 0.01 Hz responses come from the packet Baler, which decimates these channels from the Q330S+ 1 Hz data stream.
