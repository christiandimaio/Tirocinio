Tap Table Lookups Numbers

The DM24 generates sample rates for each channel by cascading decimation filters to create up to 6 successive "taps" or sample rates. Some or all of these sample rates may be output.

The FIR filters used for a given sample rate is determined by the tap sequence programmed into the datalogger at startup time. Each unique combination of taps is referenced by a "Tap Table Lookup (TTL) Number". Knowning this number is necessary to locate the correct instrument response, since a single sample rate can be generated in a variety of ways. The TTL can be found in the GCF header of the DM24 native format trace data.
