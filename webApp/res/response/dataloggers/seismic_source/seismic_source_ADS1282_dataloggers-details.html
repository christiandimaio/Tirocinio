The DAQ3-3, Sigma 3 and Sigma 4 datalogger models all use the TI ADS1282 Analog-To-Digital Converter and have the same responses. Each offers preamp gain options of x1 and x16, but digitized count values are scaled by the inverse of this setting before samples exit the datalogger.
