Johnson infraBSU Infrasound Sensor Responses

infraBSU Sensitivity and Low-frequency Corner

The infraBSU uses a MEMS analog differential transducer whose sensitivity within the passband is mesured using hydrostatic pressure:

MEMS water level Sensitivity
0.5 inches 46 microV/Pa
1.0 inches 23 microV/Pa
5.0 inches 9.1 microV/Pa

This instrument also includes a color-coded capillary attachment that determines the low-frequency corner of the passband:

Color Capillary diameter Corner frequency
Pink 25 microns 0.016 Hz
White 50 microns 0.031 Hz
Yellow 50 microns 0.035 Hz
Blue 62.5 microns 0.048 Hz
