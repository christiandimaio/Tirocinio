Q330 HR(High Resolution A/D)

The two sensor ports (QAPs) on the Q330 are sampled by different A/Ds. Port A (channels 1-3) is sampled by a high-resolution A/D with four times the number of counts/volt as the Q330SR (standard resolution) A/D. Port B (channels 4-6) is sampled by a standard resolution A/D identical to the A/D in the Q330SR. The Q330HR preamp offers gains of 1 or 20.
