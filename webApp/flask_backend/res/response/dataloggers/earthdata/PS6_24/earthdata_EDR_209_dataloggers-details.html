Note: The EDR-209 and EDR-210 dataloggers have the same instrument responses. The EDR-210 incorporates internal or swappable storage media, wherease the EDR-209 does not. Both EDR dataloggers also have the same instrument responses as the PS6-24 and PR6-24 models with the exception that later models of the EDR-209 and 210 have a fourth gain option "Very High" (20nV/bit)
