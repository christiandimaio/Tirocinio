Note: The Basalt, Basalt 4X/8X, Granite, Dolomite and Obsidian dataloggers have the same responses and default A/D scale factors (counts/V). The 20 Hz sample rate is only available on the Granite datalogger. The 2000 Hz sample rate is only available on the Basalt and Obsidian dataloggers. The 5000 Hz sample rate is only found on the Obsidian.
